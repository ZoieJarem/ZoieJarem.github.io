Art is not only a pashion of mine, but it is also my life. There is not a single day where I am not thinking about a new project and how to start it. I have always loved all types of animals, no matter the size or how many teeth that they have. For me, that is why I tend to base the majority of my work off of many species of animals. I do tend to do other peices of art that do not include the animal kingdome.
