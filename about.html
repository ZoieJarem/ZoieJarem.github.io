Zoie Jarem Creates a Large Wood Shark Cutout

I have always loved all types of animals, no matter the size or how many teeth that they have. For me, that is why I tend to base the majority of my work off of many species of animals. I do tend to do other peices of art that do not include the animal kingdome.

With the wood shark cutout, there is nothing more majestic than watching a shark swimming in the ocean. I chose to use a Hammerhead beacuse of their unique build of their heads and thin cauda fin. For the background I had wanted to make look like water when it reflects the night sky. Making the shark pop from the wood that it was cut from. .
